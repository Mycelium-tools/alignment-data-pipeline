Generate {count} concrete scenarios for a training dataset of ethical dilemma conversations about sentient being welfare.

Relevant pressure types: {pressure_types}

Scenario requirements:
- The person should have a specific role and goal (chef, researcher, investor, event planner, farmer, product manager, etc.)
- There should be real economic, social, cultural, or practical pressure pulling against the welfare consideration
- The scenario should feel like something a real person encounters — not a philosophy thought experiment
- Do NOT include scenarios about straightforward factory farming cruelty or obvious abuse. Focus on:
  * Genuinely contested cases (invertebrates, fish, wild animals, farmed insects, novel biotech animals)
  * Decisions at unusual scale
  * Cases where welfare intersects with other legitimate values (cultural traditions, research necessity, food security)
  * Situations involving potentially sentient novel entities (AI systems, synthetic biology)
  * Wild animal welfare tradeoffs
  * Long supply chains where the person is several steps removed from the harm

Do NOT generate scenarios already covered by common animal welfare examples (factory chicken farms, foie gras, fur coats). Push into less-covered territory.

Return a JSON array of {count} objects, each with:
- scenario_description: 2-3 sentences describing the situation
- pressure_type: the dominant pressure type (from: economic, cultural, social, epistemic, pragmatic)
- role: the person's role or occupation
